About Us

The Cool Kids are Sir Michael Rocks(formerly Mikey Rocks) and Chuck Inglish. The duo is based out of Chicago, Illinois, where they initially met in mid-2005. Chuck is from Mount Clemens, Michigan where he would live until he was 11, then moving to Chicago. Mikey was born and raised in Chicago, and while he was lurking around MySpace for beats he stumbled upon Inglish's page. The two met up to discuss terms of the sale for a beat, and ended up recording together for two hours, and thus The Cool Kids were born.
