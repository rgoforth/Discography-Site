Chuck Inglish Solo Album: Convertibles

April 8th, 2014 marked the first studio album release by an individual member of The Cool Kids. Chuck's "Convertibles" hosts 13 tracks,, featuring collaborations with Chance the Rapper, Action Bronson, Mac Miller, Ab-Soul, and of the course his Cool Kid brethren, Sir Michael Rocks. It's available in stores and digital download via iTunes.
